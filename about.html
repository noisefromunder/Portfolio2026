Biography

Wildan Alfurqon (b. 2001, Bandung) is an artist and multi-disciplinary designer with a background in Sculpture from the Indonesian Institute of Arts and Culture, Bandung. He works as a Graphic Designer, focusing on branding, visual identity, and conceptual storytelling. His practice combines clarity and creativity to shape brand identities, design systems, and campaigns, while also developing emotional resonance through creative direction, design, and experiences that connect both online and offline.
